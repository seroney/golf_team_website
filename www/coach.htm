Jim Burke, PGA

I am a PGA Professional at the Leo J. Martin Golf Course in Weston, which is fifteen minutes from campus. Before getting into the golf business nine years ago I attend the Univ.of Maine where I played on the hockey team. I then went on to play three years professionally after graduating. My experience as a student athlete as well as a professional athlete has taught me the dedication and commitment level it takes to excel. I feel my greatest asset, as a coach is my ability to teach golfers of all levels the game of golf both physically and mentally. I understand the academic commitment of the MIT student and emphasize that always come first. I look for golfers who demonstrate the desire to learn and improve and who represent the high standards to which MIT is committed both on the course and in the classroom.
