Welcome !

You have reached the Engineers' homepage, that is, the MIT Varsity Golf Team. Our team competes in the Constitution Conference of the NCAA Division III.
The golf team is one of the 39 varsity teams (24 for men, 15 for women) in existence at MIT. You can visit the Department of Athletic's homepage to know more about the sports played at the Institute.

The Engineers play a combined Fall/Spring schedule. Some of the teams we usually compete with during the regular season are Bentley College, Boston College, Boston University, Bowdoin College, Brandeis University, Merrimack College, Northeastern University, Tufts University, Worcester Polytechnic Institute, and Worcester State College.


If you are, or will be, an MIT student next fall and would like to try out the golf team, please contact our coaches for an appointment.
If you are a visitor to these pages, please enjoy the golf tradition at MIT as you navegate over the different sections of our site. Thanks for visiting !!
